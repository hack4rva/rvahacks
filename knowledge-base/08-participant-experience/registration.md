## Team Registration

### Individual Registration
- Register yourself first
- Find a team on-site (Friday team formation)
- Join a team on Devpost after the event starts

### Team Registration
- Have a team already? Great!
- One person creates the team on Devpost
- Team members join with team code
- Team size: 2-5 people recommended

---

## Minors Policy

Participants under 18 are welcome with:
- Parent/guardian consent form (signed)
- Emergency contact information
- Must be at least 14 years old

Minors cannot participate in overnight satellite hacking without a parent/guardian present.

---

## Accessibility

Need accommodations? Indicate during registration:
- Mobility assistance
- Dietary restrictions (allergies, kosher, halal, vegan, etc.)
- Sensory accommodations
- Service animal
- Other (please specify)

We'll reach out to coordinate specifics.

## Confirmation & Check-In

### After Registration
- Confirmation email within 24 hours
- Pre-event emails with logistics and updates
- Discord invite link

### Day-Of Check-In
- Bring valid ID matching registration name
- Check in at Registration desk (Main Hall)
- Receive badge and swag bag
- You're in!
