# What to Bring to Hack for RVA

**Last Updated:** December 2024

---

## Packing Checklist

### Required Items

- [ ] **Valid ID** — Required for check-in
- [ ] **Laptop** — Your primary work machine
- [ ] **Laptop charger** — You'll need it!
- [ ] **Phone + charger** — Stay connected
- [ ] **Any medications** — Bring what you need

### Highly Recommended

- [ ] **Headphones** — For focus time and video calls
- [ ] **Mouse** — If you prefer one
- [ ] **Notebook + pen** — For brainstorming
- [ ] **Water bottle** — Stay hydrated
- [ ] **Snacks** — We provide meals, but personal snacks help
- [ ] **Layers** — Venues can vary in temperature
- [ ] **Comfortable shoes** — You'll be on your feet

### For Overnight Hackers

If you're using satellite locations for overnight hacking:

- [ ] **Sleeping bag / blanket** — Sleep spaces are informal
- [ ] **Pillow** — Get some rest
- [ ] **Toiletries** — Toothbrush, deodorant, etc.
- [ ] **Change of clothes** — Fresh start on day 2/3
- [ ] **Eye mask + earplugs** — Sleep aids

### Optional But Helpful

- [ ] **External monitor** — If you have a portable one
- [ ] **USB hub** — Extra ports
- [ ] **Extension cord / power strip** — Be a team hero
- [ ] **Presentation clicker** — For demo day
- [ ] **Business cards** — Networking happens

---

## What NOT to Bring

- ❌ **Alcohol** — It's a family-friendly event
- ❌ **Weapons** — Obviously not allowed
- ❌ **Large speakers** — Headphones only
- ❌ **Pets** — Service animals welcome with advance notice
- ❌ **Bad attitude** — Come ready to collaborate!

## Pre-Event Digital Prep

Before the hackathon, set up your digital environment:

### Required Accounts (Free)
- [ ] **GitHub** — For code collaboration
- [ ] **Devpost** — For team registration and submission
- [ ] **Discord** — For event communication

### Recommended Setup
- [ ] Install your preferred development environment
- [ ] Test your laptop's webcam and mic (for virtual aspects)
- [ ] Download any large files/tools you might need (WiFi gets congested)
- [ ] Have your portfolio/resume accessible (networking opportunity!)

## Health & Wellness

### Rest
- Pace yourself — 48 hours is a marathon
- Sleep areas available at satellite locations
- Don't skip meals

### Comfort
- Stretch regularly
- Take breaks
- Get fresh air

### If You Feel Unwell
- Notify a volunteer
- Quiet/rest space available
- We can call medical assistance if needed
