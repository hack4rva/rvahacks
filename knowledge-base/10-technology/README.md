## City Data & APIs

### Richmond Open Data Portal
- **URL:** [data.richmondgov.com](https://data.richmondgov.com)
- **Contents:** Public datasets from city departments

### Available Datasets (Examples)
| Dataset | Department | Format |
|---------|------------|--------|
| 311 Service Requests | Public Works | CSV/API |
| Building Permits | Development | CSV/API |
| Crime Statistics | Police | CSV |
| Budget Data | Finance | CSV |
| Transit Routes | GRTC | GTFS |

### Special Access
Some datasets may be made available specifically for the hackathon. Coordinate through the City Liaison (Michael Kolbe).
