## The 7 Pillars (Mayor's Action Plan)

Each pillar represents a major area of civic improvement from Mayor Avula's Action Plan. Teams choose a pillar and build solutions addressing real challenges.

### Pillar 1: Thriving City Hall
**Focus:** Modernizing government operations and citizen services
- Digital transformation
- Transparency and open data
- Citizen engagement tools

### Pillar 2: Thriving Neighborhoods
**Focus:** Housing, community development, and neighborhood vitality
- Affordable housing solutions
- Neighborhood safety
- Community investment tools

### Pillar 3: Thriving Families
**Focus:** Education, youth development, and family services
- Educational equity
- Youth programming
- Family support services

### Pillar 4: Thriving Economy
**Focus:** Economic development and workforce opportunities
- Small business support
- Workforce development
- Economic mobility tools

### Pillar 5: Inclusive Communities
**Focus:** Equity, accessibility, and community inclusion
- Health equity
- Accessibility improvements
- Social services access

### Pillar 6: Thriving Environment
**Focus:** Environmental sustainability and resilience
- Climate resilience
- Green infrastructure
- Environmental justice

### Pillar 7: City Stories
**Focus:** Arts, culture, and Richmond's heritage
- Cultural preservation
- Public art and expression
- Heritage tourism

## Challenge Design Framework

Each challenge should include:

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Problem Statement** | Clear description of the civic problem |
| **Impact** | Why this matters to Richmond |
| **Stakeholders** | Who is affected |
| **Data Available** | What data/APIs exist |
| **Success Criteria** | What a good solution looks like |
| **Implementation Path** | How winners could see adoption |

---

## Judging Criteria

Submissions are judged on:

| Criterion | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| **Impact** | 30% | Potential to improve Richmond |
| **Feasibility** | 25% | Can this actually be built/deployed? |
| **Innovation** | 20% | Creative approach to the problem |
| **Presentation** | 15% | Clear communication of solution |
| **Civic Alignment** | 10% | How well it addresses pillar goals |
