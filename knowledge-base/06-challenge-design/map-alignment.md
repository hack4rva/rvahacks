# Mayor's Action Plan Alignment

**Last Updated:** December 2024

---

## Overview

Hack for RVA challenges are directly aligned with **Mayor Danny Avula's Action Plan for Richmond**. Each pillar addresses specific priorities identified by the Mayor's office.

---

## The 7 Pillars

### Pillar 1: Thriving City Hall
**MAP Focus:** Modernize government operations, improve efficiency, enhance citizen services.

| Challenge Area | Potential Projects |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Digital Services | Online permit applications, payment portals |
| Data Transparency | Open data dashboards, FOIA automation |
| Citizen Engagement | 311 improvements, feedback systems |
| Internal Efficiency | Workflow automation, cross-department tools |

**City Departments:** IT, Mayor's Office, Communications, HR

---

### Pillar 2: Thriving Neighborhoods
**MAP Focus:** Safe, affordable housing and vibrant communities.

| Challenge Area | Potential Projects |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Housing Access | Affordable housing finders, rent assistance tools |
| Community Safety | Neighborhood watch apps, lighting analysis |
| Blight Reduction | Vacant property tracking, remediation prioritization |
| Investment Tracking | Development project visibility |

**City Departments:** Housing, Community Development, Police, Public Works

---

### Pillar 3: Thriving Families
**MAP Focus:** Support for families through education and services.

| Challenge Area | Potential Projects |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Education Access | School choice tools, tutoring matching |
| Youth Programs | Activity finders, enrollment systems |
| Family Services | Resource navigation, benefits access |
| Childcare | Availability mapping, subsidy calculators |

**City Departments:** Education (RPS liaison), Social Services, Recreation

---

### Pillar 4: Thriving Economy
**MAP Focus:** Economic opportunity and workforce development.

| Challenge Area | Potential Projects |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Small Business | Permit navigators, funding finders |
| Workforce | Job matching, skills training connection |
| Entrepreneurship | Startup resources, mentor matching |
| SWaM Support | Minority business certification, procurement access |

**City Departments:** Economic Development, Procurement, Workforce Dev

---

### Pillar 5: Inclusive Communities
**MAP Focus:** Equity, health access, and community inclusion.

| Challenge Area | Potential Projects |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Health Equity | Clinic finders, health resource mapping |
| Accessibility | Transit accessibility, ADA compliance tools |
| Language Access | Translation services, multilingual resources |
| Social Services | Benefits enrollment, case management |

**City Departments:** Health, Social Services, Human Services, Equity

---

### Pillar 6: Thriving Environment
**MAP Focus:** Environmental sustainability and climate resilience.

| Challenge Area | Potential Projects |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Climate Resilience | Flood mapping, heat island analysis |
| Green Infrastructure | Tree inventory, stormwater management |
| Waste Management | Recycling optimization, bulk pickup scheduling |
| Energy Efficiency | Building energy audits, solar potential |

**City Departments:** Public Utilities, Sustainability, Public Works

---

### Pillar 7: City Stories
**MAP Focus:** Arts, culture, and Richmond's unique heritage.

| Challenge Area | Potential Projects |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Heritage Preservation | Historical site documentation, oral histories |
| Public Art | Mural mapping, public art tours |
| Cultural Events | Event discovery, community calendar |
| Tourism | Visitor guides, heritage trails |

**City Departments:** Arts & Culture, Tourism, Libraries, Museums

---

## Implementation KPIs

Each pillar tracks implementation success through:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Solutions Submitted** | Number of teams working on pillar |
| **Quality Score** | Average judge score for pillar submissions |
| **Department Match** | City department agrees to explore implementation |
| **90-Day Plan** | Winner creates implementation roadmap |
| **Pilot Launch** | Solution deployed in pilot mode |
| **Full Implementation** | Solution integrated into city operations |
