## Impact Categories

### 1. Event Execution
Did we run a successful event?

| KPI | Target | How Measured |
|-----|--------|--------------|
| Participant count | 300+ | Registration |
| Team submissions | 25+ | Devpost |
| Participant satisfaction | 4.5/5 | Post-event survey |
| Net Promoter Score | 50+ | Post-event survey |

### 2. Solution Quality
Did teams build good solutions?

| KPI | Target | How Measured |
|-----|--------|--------------|
| Technical completeness | 80% deployed demos | Judging review |
| Pillar coverage | All 7 pillars represented | Submission analysis |
| Innovation score | 4/5 average | Judge scoring |

### 3. Implementation
Did solutions get adopted?

| KPI | Target | How Measured |
|-----|--------|--------------|
| 90-day plans created | 3+ | Post-event tracking |
| City department matches | 3+ | Follow-up |
| Implementations started | 1+ | 6-month check-in |
| Solutions deployed | 1+ | 12-month assessment |

### 4. Community Building
Did we strengthen Richmond's civic tech ecosystem?

| KPI | Target | How Measured |
|-----|--------|--------------|
| New connections made | 500+ | Post-event survey |
| Cross-sector teams | 50%+ | Team composition |
| Return interest | 80% "would attend again" | Post-event survey |
| Ongoing engagement | 100+ Discord members | Platform analytics |

### 5. Diversity & Inclusion
Did we create an inclusive event?

| KPI | Target | How Measured |
|-----|--------|--------------|
| First-time hackers | 40%+ | Registration |
| Underrepresented participants | 40%+ | Optional demographics |
| Accessibility satisfaction | 4.5/5 | Post-event survey |

## Reporting Cadence

| Report | Audience | Timing |
|--------|----------|--------|
| **Post-Event Summary** | All stakeholders | Within 2 weeks |
| **Sponsor Report** | Sponsors | Within 4 weeks |
| **City Report** | City of Richmond | Within 4 weeks |
| **6-Month Impact Report** | All stakeholders | 6 months post-event |
| **Annual Report** | Public | 12 months post-event |

## Survey Strategy

### Participant Survey (Post-Event)
- Satisfaction ratings
- Net Promoter Score
- Suggestions for improvement
- Interest in future events
- Optional demographics

### Sponsor Survey (Post-Event)
- Goals met
- Value received
- Likelihood to sponsor again
- Feedback on logistics

### Stakeholder Interviews (Post-Event)
- Pillar leads feedback
- City department feedback
- Winner interviews
