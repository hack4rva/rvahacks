## Code of Conduct Summary

Hack for RVA is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone. We do not tolerate harassment in any form.

### Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and inclusive
- Collaborate constructively
- Help create a welcoming environment
- Follow event rules and instructions

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Offensive comments related to identity
- Unwelcome physical contact
- Disruption of the event
- Photography without consent

### Reporting & Enforcement
- Report to any organizer immediately
- All reports treated confidentially
- Violators may be removed without refund

## Intellectual Property Policy

### Default Position
- **Participants retain ownership** of their code and ideas
- Open-source encouraged but not required
- City of Richmond may request permission to implement winning solutions

### Requirements
- Must disclose any pre-existing IP used
- Cannot submit copyrighted material without license
- Submissions may be demoed publicly
