# Event Format & Schedule

**Last Updated:** December 2024
**Event Dates:** March 27-29, 2026

---

## Overview

Hack for RVA is a **48-hour civic hackathon** with multiple ways to participate. Whether you can commit the full weekend or just a few hours, there's a path for you.

---

## Event Format

### Duration
- **Start:** Friday, March 27, 2026 @ 9:00 AM
- **End:** Sunday, March 29, 2026 @ 4:00 PM
- **Hacking Time:** ~48 hours

### Locations
- **Primary Venue:** Science Museum of Virginia (Gottwald Center)
- **Satellite Locations:** University of Richmond, VCU, and more (for overnight hacking)

## Pillar Breakout Sessions (Friday)

Each of the 7 Mayor's Action Plan pillars has a 90-minute breakout session:

1. **Thriving City Hall** — Government modernization challenges
2. **Thriving Neighborhoods** — Housing and community development
3. **Thriving Families** — Education and family services
4. **Thriving Economy** — Workforce and economic development
5. **Inclusive Communities** — Equity and accessibility
6. **Thriving Environment** — Sustainability and resilience
7. **City Stories** — Arts, culture, and heritage

Each session includes:
- 15 min: City Hall Owner presentation
- 15 min: Nonprofit Partner perspective
- 5 min: Corporate Sponsor context
- 30 min: Q&A and team formation
- 15 min: Break and networking
