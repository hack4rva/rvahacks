# Role Cards: Saturday Volunteers

## Overview

Saturday is the distributed hacking day with teams working at 15+ satellite locations across Richmond. Volunteers are needed both at physical sites and in virtual support roles.

**Total Saturday Volunteers Needed: 60-80 people**
- Per-site roles: 45-75 people (3-5 per site Ã— 15 sites)
- Virtual/central roles: 18-25 people

---

## Site Captain

### Role Card

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **ID** | `site-captain` |
| **Category** | Saturday |
| **Subcategory** | Site-Level |
| **Location** | Assigned satellite venue |
| **Shift** | Full day (9:00 AM - 6:00 PM) or split with co-captain |
| **Quantity** | 1-2 per site (15-30 total) |
| **Reports To** | Satellite Location Coordinator |
| **Training Required** | 1-hr training + venue walkthrough |

### You Own

- Open venue and set up hacking space
- Greet teams as they arrive
- First point of contact for any issues at your site
- Ensure WiFi, power, and basic amenities work
- Coordinate meal/snack deliveries if applicable
- Maintain positive energy and check on team progress
- Communicate site status to central command every 2 hours
- Close venue and ensure all teams have departed safely

### You Escalate

| Situation | Escalate To | Channel |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Code of Conduct violation | Event Manager | Slack DM immediately |
| Medical emergency | 911, then Event Manager | Phone, then Slack |
| Technical problem you can't solve | Platform Administrator | Discord #tech-help |
| Team conflict or dispute | Volunteer Coordinator | Slack DM |
| Venue issue (HVAC, security, access) | Satellite Location Coordinator | Phone |
| Media or press inquiry | Social Media Manager | Slack #comms |

### Decision Authority

- Rearrange furniture to accommodate team needs
- Make judgment calls on minor venue comfort issues
- Order emergency supplies up to $50 (keep receipt)
- Adjust local schedule within site (break times, etc.)
- Grant brief extensions for site-specific technical issues

### Success Criteria

- All teams at your site submit on time
- Teams report positive experience
- No major incidents
- Site status reported every 2 hours
- Venue left in good condition

### Skills Required

- Leadership and problem-solving
- Clear communication
- Calm under pressure
- Physical ability to be on feet all day

### Skills Preferred

- Event management experience
- Technical background (helpful for troubleshooting)
- First aid certification
- Knowledge of satellite venue

### Pre-Event Checklist

- [ ] Visit venue before event day
- [ ] Get venue contact's phone number
- [ ] Know WiFi password and setup
- [ ] Identify power outlet locations
- [ ] Know nearest restrooms, parking
- [ ] Have emergency supply kit
- [ ] Confirm food delivery schedule (if applicable)

| Suspected cheating or rule violation | Event Manager | DM + document |
| Submission platform issues | Platform Administrator | #tech-help |

- Mute disruptive users for up to 10 minutes with warning
- Delete spam or clearly inappropriate content
- Create temporary channels for specific discussions
- Pin/unpin messages as needed
- Issue informal warnings for minor issues

### Success Criteria

- Questions answered within 10 minutes
- Channels stay organized and on-topic
- No unaddressed CoC issues
- Positive community atmosphere maintained

### Skills Required

- Discord/Slack proficiency
- Strong written communication
- Patience and diplomacy
- Attention to multiple channels

### Skills Preferred

- Online moderation experience
- Technical knowledge (to route questions)
