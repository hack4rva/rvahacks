# Role Cards: Friday Volunteers

## Overview

Friday volunteers support the kickoff event at the Science Museum of Virginia. This is the most structured day with pillar sessions, team formation, and participant onboarding.

**Total Friday Volunteers Needed: 35-45 people**

---

## Registration Lead

### Role Card

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **ID** | `registration-lead` |
| **Category** | Friday |
| **Location** | Science Museum of Virginia |
| **Shift** | 8:30 AM - 12:00 PM |
| **Quantity** | 3-4 |
| **Reports To** | Registration Manager |
| **Training Required** | 30-min system training |

### You Own

- Set up registration tables before doors open (8:30 AM)
- Check in registered participants (verify ID, provide badge)
- Distribute swag bags and event materials
- Handle walk-in registrations if capacity allows
- Direct participants to opening session
- Track no-shows and late arrivals
- Maintain organized queue during rush periods
- Close out registration and report final numbers

### You Escalate

| Situation | Escalate To | Channel |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Registration system technical issues | Registration Manager | In person or Slack |
| Capacity questions (at limit?) | Registration Manager | In person |
| Participant without valid registration | Registration Manager | In person |
| Media/VIP arrival | Event Manager | Radio/Slack |
| Accessibility accommodation needed | Volunteer Coordinator | In person |

### Decision Authority

- Approve minor name corrections on badges
- Provide replacement materials if participant lost theirs
- Open additional check-in lanes if queue exceeds 10 minutes
- Allow late arrivals to skip queue if session started

### Success Criteria

- Average check-in time under 2 minutes
- No participant complaints about registration
- All materials distributed
- Final count reported by noon

### Skills Required

- Customer service orientation
- Organization and attention to detail
- Ability to work quickly under pressure

### Skills Preferred

- Event registration experience
- Multilingual (Spanish helpful)

## Pillar Room Captain

### Role Card

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **ID** | `pillar-room-captain` |
| **Category** | Friday |
| **Location** | Science Museum of Virginia (assigned room) |
| **Shift** | Per session (60-90 min each) |
| **Quantity** | 14-21 total (2-3 per pillar, covering waves) |
| **Reports To** | Friday Programming Lead |
| **Training Required** | 30-min AV/facilitation training |

### You Own

- Arrive 15 min early to prep room for pillar session
- Greet City Liaison, Nonprofit envoy, and Corporate rep
- Confirm AV needs and test equipment with speakers
- Introduce all three speakers and explain session format
- Facilitate panel flow and transitions
- Manage Q&A (hand mic to questioners, balance participation)
- Give time warnings (10-min, 5-min, 1-min)
- Ensure recording equipment is running
- Clear room after session for next use

### You Escalate

| Situation | Escalate To | Channel |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Speaker no-show 5 min before start | Friday Programming Lead | Phone immediately |
| AV equipment failure | AV Runner | Radio |
| Room capacity exceeded | Event Manager | Radio |
| Stakeholder conflict | Event Manager | Discreetly, in person |
| Medical emergency | Event Manager + 911 | Radio immediately |

### Decision Authority

- Extend Q&A by up to 5 minutes if schedule allows
- Rearrange seating for accessibility needs
- Pause session briefly for technical fix (< 2 min)
- Diplomatically redirect off-topic or overly long questions
- Adjust room temperature if controls accessible

### Success Criteria

- Session starts and ends on time
- All three stakeholder voices heard equally
- Recording captures everything
- Participants leave with clear understanding of pillar challenges
- Positive feedback from speakers

### Skills Required

- Public speaking comfort
- Time management
- Assertiveness (diplomatic but firm)
- Basic AV troubleshooting

### Skills Preferred

- Facilitation experience
- Domain knowledge in session topic
- Previous event MC experience
