# Role Cards: Sunday Volunteers

## Overview

Sunday is Finals and Awards day at the Science Museum of Virginia. This is a public event with live pitches, judging, and celebration. It's family-friendly and open to the community.

**Total Sunday Volunteers Needed: 30-40 people**

---

## Setup Crew

### Role Card

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **ID** | `setup-crew` |
| **Category** | Sunday |
| **Location** | Science Museum of Virginia |
| **Shift** | 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM |
| **Quantity** | 4-6 |
| **Reports To** | Event Manager |
| **Training Required** | 15-min venue orientation |

### You Own

- Arrive early to set up venue for finals
- Arrange seating for audience (general and reserved sections)
- Set up signage and wayfinding
- Prepare stage area (podium, screens, backdrop)
- Set up finalist check-in and green room area
- Coordinate with AV team on equipment placement
- Prepare registration/welcome table

### You Escalate

| Situation | Escalate To | Channel |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Venue access issues | Science Museum Liaison | Phone |
| Missing equipment or supplies | Event Manager | Slack |
| Safety concerns | Event Manager | In person |
| AV setup issues | Stage Manager | In person |

### Decision Authority

- Seating arrangement within guidelines
- Minor adjustments to room layout
- Where to place signage

### Success Criteria

- Venue ready for check-in by 10 AM
- All signage visible and correct
- Stage area presentable
- No last-minute scrambles

### Skills Required

- Physical ability to move furniture
- Attention to detail
- Ability to follow setup diagram

### Skills Preferred

- Event setup experience
- Early morning availability
- Knowledge of Science Museum

## Timekeeper

### Role Card

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **ID** | `timekeeper` |
| **Category** | Sunday |
| **Location** | Science Museum of Virginia (near stage) |
| **Shift** | During pitches (approx 2-3 hours) |
| **Quantity** | 2 (primary + backup) |
| **Reports To** | Friday Programming Lead |
| **Training Required** | 15-min timing protocol review |

### You Own

- Display time remaining visibly to presenter
- Give visual cues at 2 minutes, 1 minute, 30 seconds remaining
- Signal hard stop at time limit
- Track actual time used by each team
- Ensure consistent enforcement across all teams
- Coordinate with Stage Manager on transitions

### You Escalate

| Situation | Escalate To | Channel |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Team significantly over time despite warnings | Stage Manager | Visual signal |
| Technical issue during pitch affecting time | Stage Manager | In person |
| Judge asks to extend Q&A | Judge Coordinator | In person |
| Presenter dispute about timing | Event Manager | In person, after pitch |

### Decision Authority

- Grant up to 15 extra seconds for obvious technical issues
- Pause timer during clear AV problems (with Stage Manager approval)
- Adjust warning intervals for different pitch lengths

### Success Criteria

- All teams get equal time
- Schedule stays on track
- No complaints about fairness
- Clear, visible time signals

### Skills Required

- Attention to detail
- Assertiveness (must enforce time limits)
- Fairness-minded
- Ability to stay focused

### Skills Preferred

- Event or competition timing experience
- Stopwatch/timer proficiency
- Stage visibility experience
