## Sponsorship Tiers

### Title Sponsor ($50,000+)
- Naming rights
- Logo on all materials
- Speaking opportunity at opening
- VIP judging role
- Dedicated recruitment booth

### Pillar Sponsor ($10,000+)
- Pillar naming rights
- Logo on pillar materials
- Judging role for pillar
- Recruitment access

### Supporting Sponsor ($5,000+)
- Logo on website and signage
- Recruitment table access
- Social media mentions

### Community Partner (In-kind)
- Logo on website
- Acknowledgment during event
- Volunteer coordination
